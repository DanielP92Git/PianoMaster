### Pedagogical Principles (data restructure)

- [x] **REQ-01**: Pulse-first ordering principle — quarter note is the first rhythmic-content node a learner encounters (after any intro/scaffolding); halves and wholes are introduced as extensions of the pulse; eighths and sixteenths as subdivisions — **✅ satisfied** (enforced by `validateConcept`/`validateOrder` lint rules; trail first content node = `rhythm_1_1` = `['q']`)
- [x] **REQ-02**: Rests-woven principle — each rest type (quarter, half, whole rest) is introduced in or immediately adjacent to its matching duration unit; no aggregate "Rests" unit — **✅ satisfied**
- [x] **REQ-03**: Concept-per-unit principle — no unit mixes two distinct learning concepts; meters live in dedicated meter units; aggregate bosses sit in their own review unit — **✅ satisfied** (U10 single cumulative review boss per D-11)

### Scaffolding & Engine
