    - Prerequisites: Device volume up, logged in
    - Steps: (1) Navigate to any game that plays piano tones (e.g., rhythm dictation, or note recognition), (2) Trigger a note play (start a rhythm dictation question, or answer in note recognition), (3) Listen to the tone through device speakers
    - Expected: Piano-like tone (not buzzy, not silent, recognizable as piano). Especially important on iOS Safari where AudioContext may be suspended.

    - Prerequisites: App installed as PWA on device from `https://my-pianomaster.netlify.app`, NOT localhost (per research pitfall 3)
