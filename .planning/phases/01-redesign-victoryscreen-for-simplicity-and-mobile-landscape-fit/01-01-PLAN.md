<objective>
Redesign VictoryScreen for content simplicity and mobile landscape fit.

Purpose: The current VictoryScreen is a 1105-line monolithic component that overflows on mobile landscape viewports (280-400px height). It shows too much information for an 8-year-old audience (XP breakdowns, percentile comparisons, progress bars, points cards). This plan extracts business logic into a reusable hook, then rebuilds the render layer as a two-panel landscape layout with only essential content: avatar, stars, celebration message, XP line, and action buttons.

Output: A `useVictoryState` hook containing all business logic (unchanged behavior) and a simplified `VictoryScreen.jsx` with two-panel landscape layout that fits 320px viewports without scrolling.
</objective>
